School Projects

My Vision

I want my designs to offer people a new lens through which they can view life. Something that not only helps them, but also challenges their assumptions and invites them to question what they once considered absolute truth. Through my work, I aim to create meaningful experiences that inspire reflection, curiosity, and a deeper understanding of the world around them.

The Dream

This was a project that I wanted to do for years where I painted my own skateboard with a theme of things that meant a lot to me. Unfortunately it failed as a school project but I’m still happy with the end result.

The Room of Surrealism

Every year we have 2 weeks where we can make whatever we want inside a theme or idea. This time it was about telling the story behind a painting and telling it in a new way. We chose the painting The persistence of memory by Salvador Dali, and made a room that makes it feel like you are in his painting.

F*ck the Patriarchy

This poster was made for a project where we needed to make something that would represent us as designers. I made it with the idea of getting people to start a discussion about abortion rights, specifically in the United States, and really provoke them by what’s on the poster.

Dopamine Hit

Dopamine Hit was a project where we would try to give people an new perspective on the idea of secondhand clothing. We wanted to change the way people look at secondhand clothing, so that they don’t think it’s dirty, but that it has character.

Fidget Tools

The first design I made at Avans University was this Fidget tool. As a designer I wanted to be able to help others with their problems. My designs should help others in areas where there isn’t enough attention.

ReMind

ReMind was a project that focussed on making a design that would be something that could be used in the future. Our design was a device that would be worn as an accessory, where you would be able to remove small irritations so you would never have to have a bad day again.

The Smart Library

Our Smart Library was a design to make something that already exists into a product for the future. This library knows when you are in front of it, lights up and suggests books to you based on your profile by making it light up. When picking up a book it gives information about it in the book and showcases this on the screen on the right.

The Monster

This movie we made was about isolation and was the first movie I had ever made. It is a one minute video that uses no talking, but rather the use of ‘show, not tell’ to get the story across. More information about this project will be available at a later time.

A Crazy Robot

This robot that I made for a project was acting more crazy the longer it was stuck in a small space in isolation. We needed to make a robot and design/code its behavior to make you feel the emotions of the robot despite having facial features. My robot went more and more crazy over time the longer it was stuck in a small space alone. Only a touch of someone else could calm it down.

Smelling Music

Have you ever been to a party and the music was just too loud at some places? This was the mask I made that revolved around. I detected the decibel of the music and depending on how loud or quiet it was you would have different scents you would smell. Strong once to make you go to another spot and nice once to make you engaged more with the music.
